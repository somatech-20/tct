TB Case Tracer Management System

A modern, offline-first digital solution to transform tuberculosis contact tracing and patient management in Somalia.

Explore Features View Demo

The Challenge

Paper-Based TB Tracing in Somalia

  • Data loss due to damaged or lost notebooks
  • Reporting delays of days or weeks
  • No real-time visibility of outbreaks
  • High risk of treatment default and drug resistance
  • No geolocation for hotspot identification

Our Digital Solution

Offline-First Design

Tracers work without internet. Data syncs automatically when connectivity is restored.

Geolocation Mapping

GPS-enabled contact tracing to identify TB hotspots and allocate resources efficiently.

Automated Alerts

Real-time reminders for missed doses and follow-ups to improve treatment adherence.

Secure Patient Database

Role-based access to detailed case and contact records with encryption and privacy.

Real-Time Dashboards

Live monitoring of key indicators: cases, contacts screened, adherence rates, and more.

Mobile Web App

No app store needed. Access directly via smartphone browser—low data, fast loading.

How It Works

1

TB Diagnosis

Clinician registers index case via web app or paper (digitized later).

2

Assign Tracer

System auto-assigns a community health worker to the case.

3

Field Visit (Offline)

Tracer visits household using mobile app—records contacts, symptoms, GPS location.

4

Auto-Sync & Alerts

When online, data syncs to cloud. Missed doses trigger SMS/app alerts.

5

Dashboard Insights

Program managers view real-time maps, adherence stats, and cluster alerts.

Designed for Somalia

📍 Banaadir Focus

Piloted in Mogadishu—Somalia’s highest TB burden zone with >70% smartphone penetration.

🛡️ Privacy First

Encrypted data, consent-based GPS, and role-based access ensure patient confidentiality.

🚀 Scalable Platform

Built to expand to other regions after successful pilot—zero infrastructure cost.
